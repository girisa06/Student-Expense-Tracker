# **Student Expense Tracker**
A simple expense tracker designed for students
## How to Use
- Add your current wallet balance and the monthly allowance <br>
- Then add each expense by giving it a name, by choosing a category from the dropdown and by specifying the amount spent and click the **Add Expense** button.
- This adds the expense to a list of expenses displaying their characteristics and automatically calculates the wallet balance and money spent.
- In order to delete an expense, click on the trash symbol next to the list of added expense. This also automatically updates the balance and expenditure.
